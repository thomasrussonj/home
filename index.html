About

Thomas Russo is a personal injury attorney based in Howell, New Jersey. His practice is built around a single commitment: that people who have been hurt deserve skilled, honest representation. He takes the time to understand each case and each client — because no two situations are the same.

Rooted in
the Community

Before focusing exclusively on law, Thomas Russo served as a member of the Howell City Council in New Jersey. Public service shaped the way he works — with accountability, care, and a genuine understanding of the community he represents. That foundation carries into every case he takes on.
