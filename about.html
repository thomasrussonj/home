About Thomas Russo

Thomas
Russo

Attorney at Law  ·  Howell, New Jersey

Thomas Russo has spent his career representing people who have been injured — individuals navigating some of the hardest moments of their lives. He approaches every case with preparation, care, and a clear-eyed commitment to his clients' wellbeing.

Before practicing law, Thomas served as a member of the Howell City Council in New Jersey. That experience shaped how he works — with a deep respect for the people and the community he serves. He understands that behind every case, there is a person who deserves to be heard.

He is based in Howell, New Jersey, and represents clients throughout the region.
